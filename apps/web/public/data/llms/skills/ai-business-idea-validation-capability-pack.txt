## Core Workflow
1. Define target buyer and high-pain workflow.
2. Validate demand with real user signal, not vanity metrics.
3. Map alternatives and clear differentiation.
4. Test pricing and offer framing with constrained experiments.
5. Decide go/no-go with explicit evidence thresholds.

## Overview
This skill helps agents pressure-test business ideas before heavy build effort, reducing wasted cycles and weak launches.

## Capability Scope
- Buyer/problem framing
- Market and alternative analysis
- Offer and pricing experiment design
- Validation metric and threshold definition
- Decision memo generation

## Production Rules
- Base decisions on direct demand evidence, not social vanity metrics.
- Keep pricing tests tied to measurable user outcomes.
- Preserve a clear no-build threshold when evidence is weak.
- Re-test assumptions whenever segment or offer changes materially.

## Troubleshooting
**Issue:** Validation result is inconclusive
**Fix:** narrow segment and run higher-signal interviews/tests.

**Issue:** Competitor map is generic
**Fix:** compare by workflow outcome, not feature list.

**Issue:** Pricing feels arbitrary
**Fix:** tie price to measurable ROI or time/cost savings.

## Output Contract
1. Evidence-backed demand assessment.
2. Testable offer and pricing proposal.
3. Execution scope for first launch.
4. Go/no-go recommendation with rationale.
